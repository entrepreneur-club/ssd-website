Startup Speed Dating

5 November 2015 | Dozentenfoyer

You’re a startup and looking for a team?
You got what it takes and don’t want to waste it in a big company?
Then feel welcome to the Startup Speed Dating!

For students

Application deadline: October 27

You get the chance to meet a variety of great startups. The matching process gives you the chance to pick your favorites and meet the founders on a 6 min speed date. Once you’re on a date, the possibilities are limitless. You might get an internship, write your thesis with the company or even become a co-founder. Don’t miss this awesome opportunity to meet Zurich’s emerging startup scene and apply now. The event is free for admitted candidates and admission is madatory.

For startups

Application deadline: October 23

You get the chance to expose your enterprise, project or idea in a 30 second pitch to highly skilled, carefully selected candidates. Whether you are looking for co-founders, employees or researchers, you will find them among the 100 candidates through the matching process. You get 12 slots for speed dates to get what you are looking for. The price for participation ranges from 50 - 150 CHF. You’ll find a detailed explanation in the application form.

Neither startup nor student?

If you are a professor, partner, sponsor or an active part of the Zurich startup scene, please drop a line: kevin.klein@entrepreneur-club.org.
